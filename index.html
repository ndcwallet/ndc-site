F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is NDC Wallet?

    A P2P Digital Cryptocurrency Wallet by NDC Wallet.

  • Why should I use NDC Wallet?

    NDC transaction fees are lower with NDC Wallet than with credit cards. Also available staking and secure message (Anonymous)

  • What is NDC Staking?

    NDC Staking is the process of spending cpu power to process transactions, secure the network, keep everyone in the system synchronized.

    Wallet.dat is your private keys file. It holds your addresses and all the coins. So store on the safety place. Don't delete wallet.dat.
